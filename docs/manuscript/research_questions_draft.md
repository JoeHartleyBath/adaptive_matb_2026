# Research questions (draft)

Last updated: 2026-02-02

## Research Questions

- **RQ-A (core):** Is real-time mental workload (MWL) inference *alone* sufficient to drive effective online task adaptation when stress/arousal varies?
- **RQ1 (controller):** Does MWL-based controller performance degrade as stress/arousal increases?
- **RQ2 (contingency):** Does correct (vs control) MWL-contingent adaptation produce better regulation and outcomes, and is this advantage reduced under stress/arousal?
- **RQ3 (performance):** Does MWL-driven adaptation stabilise or improve task performance under higher stress/arousal?
- **RQ4 (acceptance):** How does stress/arousal affect trust, satisfaction, and perceived appropriateness of the adaptive system?
