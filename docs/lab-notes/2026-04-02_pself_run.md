## Post-session analysis findings

### SVM model validation

Ran offline diagnostics to understand why adaptation P(HIGH) ≈ 0 throughout phase 7.

**Within-calibration 5-fold CV:**

| Session | CV AUC | n_windows |
|---------|-------:|----------:|
| S001 | 0.780 ± 0.024 | 1829 |
| S003 | 0.764 ± 0.027 | 1648 |

S003's calibration model is healthy. Features genuinely separate HIGH from LOW in-session. The model is not the problem.

**Adaptation evaluation (S003, offline, offset = -4.98s):**

| Model | AUC | P(H)\|LOW | P(H)\|MOD | P(H)\|HIGH |
|-------|----:|----------:|----------:|-----------:|
| 3-class SVM-lin | 0.634 | 0.266 | 0.253 | 0.359 |
| Binary SVM-lin  | 0.546 | — | — | 0.358 |
| Binary LogReg   | 0.569 | — | — | 0.486 |

All models weak on adaptation data. Gap HIGH–LOW = ~0.09 (3-class SVM) to ~0.03 (others).

**Root cause — monotonic amplitude drift in adaptation recording:**

Raw EEG RMS per 60-second block in the adaptation XDF:

| Block | Condition | Time | RMS |
|-------|-----------|------|-----|
| 1 | LOW | 0–59s | 0.008557 |
| 2 | HIGH | 60–119s | 0.008712 |
| 3 | HIGH | 120–179s | 0.008814 |
| 4 | MODERATE | 180–239s | 0.008894 |
| 5 | HIGH | 240–299s | 0.009019 |
| 6 | LOW | 300–359s | 0.009116 |
| 7 | HIGH | 360–419s | 0.009228 |
| 8 | MODERATE | 420–479s | 0.009263 |

RMS increases **monotonically over time regardless of condition** — block 6 (LOW) is higher than every HIGH block before it. This is electrode impedance creeping up as sweat accumulates under the cap. Bandpower features track this drift, confounding the MWL signal. The model was trained on calibration data without this pattern, so it cannot generalise correctly to the adaptation run.

This explains both:
- Low offline AUC (0.634) despite healthy cal CV AUC (0.764)
- Near-zero P(HIGH) in real-time (estimator trained on pre-drift features; early adaptation blocks look "low" by comparison)

**Youden J threshold note:** S003's threshold (0.352) vs S001's (0.926) — this difference is expected and not a bug. Platt scaling is per-model; S003's SVM simply compresses probabilities toward the lower end of [0,1]. The threshold is internally consistent with the calibration CV results.
